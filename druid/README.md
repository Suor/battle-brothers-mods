# Proper Druid

Adds a Druid background - a hedge-mage who calls the beasts of the wild to fight at his
side, entangles his foes in roots and knits his allies' wounds back together.

- summon a beast fitting the battlefield: snow gives wolves, grassland wolves or spiders,
  forests add young schrats, steppe and hills give hyenas, deserts hyenas or snakes,
  swamps spiders or snakes
- available from level 1, growing stronger through Druid-only perks
- pure mage, or mage/fighter, or mage/bannerman druid builds
- a dedicated origin

## Perks

The tree splits into two mutually exclusive paths - the first group perk you take decides it,
and the other path greys out. This makes a Druid a supporter *or* a fighter, not both.

Nature (supporter):

- **Regrowth** - bestow a steady heal on a single living ally, doubled for animals; only one can carry it at a time
- **Hatch** - the summon recharges every other turn instead of once per battle
- **Entangling Roots** - root an enemy in place
- **Greater Beasts** - wolves answer as direwolves, hyenas come frenzied, spiders
  broodmother-sized, young schrats rise full-grown

Beast (fighter):

- **Beastform** - the gate: a one-time, permanent transformation. Physical bonuses
  (+10% Melee Skill, +10% Melee Defense, +20% Hitpoints) but no heavy shields, helmets or
  armor and no ranged weapons. Takes on a beast look when Fantasy Brothers is installed.
- **Beast Aura** - your beasts keep to your side instead of chasing the foe, and any allied
  beast near you stands fearless and emboldened
- **Beast Rage** - a stacking fury: melee damage, Resolve, Initiative and per-turn healing
  at the cost of Melee Defense; at its height you tear off your own shield and roar

Either path:

- **Venom** - your beasts' bites poison the prey - or, in Beastform, your own melee hits do

The summon yields a plain Wolf until level 5; the full biome variety opens up after that.

Plans:

- real artwork for the Beastform, Beast Aura and Beast Rage icons
- beast stat scaling with level, more beasts, balance pass
- optional Animal Companions (AC) integration for beast scaling and looks

## Compatibility

Standalone - summons the game's own beast entities, no hard dependency on Animal
Companions. Safe to add midgame. Works with Reforged.
