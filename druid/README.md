# Proper Druid

Adds a Druid background - a hedge-mage who calls the beasts of the wild to fight at his side.
He may walk the path of Nature, entangling his foes in roots and knitting his allies' wounds
back together, or take the beast's own shape to fight tooth and claw at the head of his pack.

- summon a beast fitting the battlefield to fight at your side, evolving with your level and perks
- two custom perk paths: Nature (heals, roots, more and greater beasts) or
  Beast (change form and lead your pack with rage or cunning)
- a dedicated origin, The Wolf and the Bear, that starts with two druids walking different paths
- plays nice with:
    - [More Backgrounds and Events][xbe] - folds into its Druid rather than adding a second one
    - [Accessory Companions][ac] - unleashed companions join your pack under the Beast Aura
    - [Starting Perks By Background][background_perks]
    - Reforged
    - [Proper Necromancer][necro]

Russian localization included - need to install [Rosetta][] and its dependencies.


## Compatibility

Safe to add midgame, cannot remove.


## Install

Download a zip from [NexusMods][] and put it into `data` dir. Requires [ModernHooks][], [MSU][] and [stdlib][]. For the built-in Russian translation also install [Rosetta][] and the Russian translation of the game itself.
